Act as a Senior Motion Designer, Three.js Engineer, and Award-Winning Creative Director.

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT redesign the website.
Do NOT change the content, typography hierarchy, color palette, navigation, buttons, spacing, or existing layout.

The only task is to replace the current right-side hero animation with a premium animated railway visualization.

DESIGN GOAL

The website should feel like a futuristic railway technology company, not a train booking website and not a generic IoT dashboard.

The visual style should resemble:

* Apple product launches
* Rivian
* Linear
* Tesla
* Modern cinematic sci-fi interfaces

HERO ANIMATION CONCEPT

Create a large holographic train made entirely from particles, glowing points, wireframes, and animated light trails.

The train should NOT look like a realistic photo.
It should look like a digital twin, simulation, or engineering visualization.

ANIMATION SEQUENCE

When the page first loads:

1. Train Assembly Phase (0-2 seconds)

* Thousands of particles begin converging.
* A train silhouette gradually forms.
* Orange and blue particles travel along the train body.
* The effect should feel like a digital system booting up.

2. Arrival Phase (2-5 seconds)

* The train moves forward slightly toward the viewer.
* Motion blur and light trails appear.
* Headlights glow softly.
* Camera remains fixed.
* Animation should be elegant, not aggressive.

3. Idle Phase (after 5 seconds)

* Train becomes stationary.
* Small particles continue moving through the structure.
* Nodes pulse occasionally.
* Subtle energy flows along the train body.
* Headlight gently breathes.
* Very slow ambient movement only.

4. Hover Interaction

* Mouse movement creates subtle parallax.
* Train responds slightly to cursor position.
* No large rotations.
* Keep interactions sophisticated.

VISUAL REQUIREMENTS

* No rectangular card container.
* No visible borders.
* No dashboard UI.
* No labels like ACP, IoT, HW, Hub.
* No floating widgets.
* The train should occupy roughly 40-50% of the hero width.
* The animation should blend naturally into the background.

STYLE REQUIREMENTS

Use:

* Particle systems
* SVG paths

* Three.js or React Three Fiber if needed

Avoid:

* Cartoon trains

LIGHTING

* Deep navy environment.
* Orange energy trails.
* Railway-blue ambient glow.
* Soft fog.
* Cinematic depth.
* Volumetric lighting effects where possible.

PERFORMANCE

* Maintain 60 FPS.
* Lazy load heavy assets.
* Optimize particle counts.
* Mobile responsive.
* Respect reduced-motion preferences.

FINAL RESULT

The visitor should see:

A futuristic digital railway system materializing from data, moving briefly when the page loads, and then remaining as a sophisticated living hologram that subtly animates while the user reads the content.

The effect should feel like a multi-million-dollar enterprise technology website rather than a traditional railway website.
